  1. Download the mobile app according to the platform of your smartphone - iOS or Android
  2. Open the app, scan an Diagram QR-Code. (It may have been provided by someone, a book or even created on this site)
  3. The application will translate the QR-Code to a playable position or the history of a game. If it is a game, you can navigate through all the moves using the navigation controls, if it is a playable position, you can play it against the Stockfish who will be using their maximum strength.

Diagram Club is an app and a website, made with loveusing wonderful libraries like Stockfish UCI Chess Engine, ChessBoard.JS, Chess.JS, and others.
